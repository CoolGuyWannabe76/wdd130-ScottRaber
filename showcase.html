Stuck in Orbit

As astronauts on a space station orbiting earth, your mission has been going well so far, but suddenly trouble with the power limits your communications and locks you out of vital areas for your mission. With a little bit of creativity and a few manual overrides, you should be able to fix it, but time isn't on your side, and earth is counting on you!

Dungeon of Mystery

Your band of adventurers has finally found the dungeon containing the treasure you need to fulfill your quest, but as soon as you enter, the door slams shut. You find an old, decayed sign that reads “Wisdom and wit will prove your worthiness to possess the Eye of Igewix.” Just as soon as you finish reading, the floor shakes, and you can hear gears turning. Whatever's happening, it can't be good news. You and your team must work together quickly to solve the dungeon's riddle if you want to make it out alive.

Pirate's Cove

You and your crew have been captured by pirates! Fortunately, these pirates love puzzles more than they love booty, and they have left a series of clues to leave. Will you be able to follow to clues to find where the X marks the spot?
